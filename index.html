Info about Ember

Our pick of what info you need to know before release and why you should choose us.

Will we offer free servers, and what will the specs be?

A main goal for us is to have quality 24/7 free servers with high specs, the image on the right shows you the unconfirmed specs we aim to provide for our Minecraft free servers, shown over 4 different plans. But this won't be all as we plan to have free plans for lots of different games! Upon release each user can choose one plan but whenever they need to upgrade and downgrade, they can switch whichever plan fits their needs.

What games will we host?

For both our free and premium plans we plan to have a plan for games such as G-Mod, Rust, Minecraft, ARK and more, as well as like alot of hosts with discord bot plans etc.

What will the price range of our premium servers be?

Unlike most hosts, we plan to have amazingly cheaper plans that have the utmost quality to support a massive amount of players. We estimate our plans will be around 0.75$-1$ per GB, this will be the same for no matter what game you are choosing to host on our services.

When do we plan to release?

We plan to release within the next 2 - 3 weeks, this may seem like a while but we are preparing some machines to handle high amounts of free servers and keep them quality and online 24/7. If you have any questions about this, join the discord.
